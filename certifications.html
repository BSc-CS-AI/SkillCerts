How to complete a course, generate certificate and post it on LinkedIn

Step 1: Log in or Sign Up for LinkedIn

Go to linkedin.com. Complete your profile to track learning and showcase certificates.

Step 2: Access LinkedIn Learning

Click on "Learning" in the top menu, or go to linkedin.com/learning.

Step 3: Browse or Search for a Course

Use the search bar or filter by topic, duration, and skill level. Popular topics include Python, Cybersecurity, AI, Resume Writing, and Time Management.

Step 4: Start the Course

Click the course title and begin watching. You can adjust speed, use subtitles, and take notes. Follow the video modules in sequence.

Step 5: Complete the Entire Course

You must watch all the videos to unlock your certificate. LinkedIn tracks your progress automatically.

Step 6: Download Your Certificate

After 100% completion, click ‚ÄúView Certificate‚Äù. Options include:

  • Download as PDF
  • Copy public URL to share
  • Add to resume or website
Step 7: Add Certificate to Your LinkedIn Profile

Click ‚ÄúAdd to profile‚Äù on the certificate page. It will be added to your Licenses & Certifications section with all necessary details filled in.
